# Shopping List Reminder

A mobile-friendly shopping list app that runs in your browser.

## Open On This Computer

Double-click `start-shopping-app.bat`, then open:

```text
http://127.0.0.1:8080/
```

## Open On Your Phone

Keep this computer and your phone on the same Wi-Fi, then open:

```text
http://192.168.1.12:8080/
```

On iPhone, open the link in Safari, tap Share, then choose Add to Home Screen.

Your list is saved in the browser on the device you use.
